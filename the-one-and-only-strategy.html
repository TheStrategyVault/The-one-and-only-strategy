Standard Operating Procedure: Advanced Dragon Tower Strategy

This Bluebook outlines a systematic protocol for engaging with the "Dragon Tower" game. The strategy is centered on disciplined session management, specific betting parameters, and operational security measures to mitigate detection. We aim for a small, pre-defined win and then immediately withdraw. This minimizes risk and avoids the catastrophic losses that come from greed or chasing losses.

Success here isn't a single jackpot. It's the consistent discipline to execute a plan: get in, achieve the objective, and get out. Don't be a hopeful gambler.

1.0 Account & Security Prerequisites

To mitigate detection and maintain operational integrity, the following setup is mandatory for each session.

  • Geographic Compliance: This strategy is designed for the global (.com) version of the platform and is not viable on the .us domain.
  • Account Generation: A new, single-session account (burner account) must be created for every execution of this strategy. Do not reuse accounts.
  • Email Anonymization: Utilize a service such as iCloud's "Hide My Email" to generate a unique email address for each new account registration.
  • IP Address Management: It is imperative to use a distinct IP address for each session.
  • Account Purity: The strategy must be deployed on a fresh account with no prior gameplay history. Specifically, the Dragon Tower game must not have been played on the account before commencing this procedure.
  • Funding: All deposits must be made exclusively via cryptocurrency.

2.0 Capital Management & Bet Calculation

Proper capital allocation is critical to the strategy's execution. Determine your total gambling budget. Calculate the starting bet by multiplying your budget by 0.002. For example:

  • Budget of $1,500 → Starting bet: $1,500 × 0.002 = $3.
  • Budget of $300 → Starting bet: $300 × 0.002 = $0.60.
initial betting size = total session budget × 0.002

3.0 Execution Protocol

Adhere strictly to the following in-game procedure.

  • Game Configuration: Set the Dragon Tower game difficulty to Hard. This setting provides the required 1.96x payout multiplier, which is a critical component of the strategy's mathematical model.
  • Automated Betting Progression: Enable the "Auto" betting feature. Navigate to the "Advanced" settings within the auto-bet panel. Configure the progression rules as follows:
    • On Loss: Set the "Increase bet by:" value to 100%.
    • On Win: Ensure the option to "Reset to base bet" is enabled. This will automatically revert the bet amount to your initial betting amount after any win.
  • Tile Selection Cycle: To introduce a degree of pattern variance, manually change your selected tile after every 100 bets have been placed (extremely important).

4.0 Risk Management & Session Control

Discipline is paramount. The following rules are designed to manage risk and must be strictly followed.

  • Session Duration Limit: Do not run any single session for more than 20 minutes. Statistical analysis done by our team indicates a significantly increased risk of catastrophic loss beyond this timeframe (aka account getting busted).
  • Profit Target: Define a clear profit target before you begin (e.g., $50 - $100), scaled according to your session budget. For smaller budgets, aim for smaller, more attainable profit targets.
  • Cease-Profit Mandate: Once the predetermined profit target is achieved, cease all gameplay immediately.
  • Immediate Withdrawal: Upon reaching your profit target, withdraw the full account balance without delay.

Principle of Discipline: Greed is the primary point of failure. Adhering to the established profit targets and session limits is non-negotiable for this strategy.
